# Noether's theorem (sketch)

**Field:** `physics` · **id:** `phys-noether`

## Statement

A continuous symmetry of the action yields a conserved current; time-translation ⇒ energy, space-translation ⇒ momentum.

## Proof sketch

1) Infinitesimal symmetry: δq=ε K(q,t) leaves S invariant up to a total time derivative.
2) On-shell, the EL identity rearranges to d/dt(p·K − F)=0.
3) That density is the Noether charge.

## Applications

- Identify invariants before solving dynamics
- Check numerical schemes for spurious drift of conserved quantities
