# Euler–Lagrange equations

**Field:** `physics` · **id:** `phys-lagrangian`

## Statement

Stationarity of S[q]=∫ L(q,q̇,t) dt implies d/dt(∂L/∂q̇)=∂L/∂q for each coordinate.

## Proof sketch

1) Vary q→q+εη with η vanishing at endpoints.
2) δS=∫(∂L/∂q η + ∂L/∂q̇ η̇)dt = 0.
3) Integrate by parts on the η̇ term; arbitrary η ⇒ Euler–Lagrange.

## Applications

- Derive equations of motion from energy functionals
- Constrained optimization via extended Lagrangians
